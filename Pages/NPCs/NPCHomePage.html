What are NPCs?

For all intents and purposes NPCs are characters. If a character is controlled by the GM, then they are NPCs or non-player-characters. If a player controls the character, then it’s a PC or player-character.

For simplicity PCs will be treated as NPCs.

If the GM controls the character, then it will be listed as “GM” otherwise, players initials will be listed in the info box.
